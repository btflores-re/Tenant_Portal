General Info

This is the tenant portal for the Strickland Bridge property in Fayetteville, NC. Via the rental page you can pay rent from your phone or submit a maintence request via the maintenance page.

Please note that rent is due on the 1st of the month, if it is not paid by COB on the 5th then a late fee (per your lease) will be charged to you.

Rent

There are a few ways you can pay rent. You can use:
1. Venmo
2. Cash App
3. Zelle Pay

VENMO

Direct Venmo Payment Link (If you are on your Smart Phone)

All you need to do is use your smartphone and take a picture of the Venmo QR Code above or click the red "Direct Venmo Payment Link" above. This will bring up the venmo app. If you have an account you will just send your rent amount to the account that pops up.
Note: Bank account payments and debit cards have no service fees and are free. If you choose to pay by credit card there is a 3% fee, so I recommend either using a debit card or your bank account!

CASH APP

Direct Cash App Payment Link (If you are on your Smart Phone)

All you need to do is use your smartphone and take a picture of the Cash App QR Code above or click the red "Direct Cash App Payment Link" above. This will bring up the Cash App application. If you have an account you will just send your rent amount to the account that pops up.

ZELLE PAY

The last way to pay rent is using ZellePay and sending the payment to: btflores93@gmail.com
